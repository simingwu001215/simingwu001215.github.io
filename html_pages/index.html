Tornadoes In the United State In 2021

The report demonstrates the situation of tornadoes by Width, Degree of Severity, and Time.

Map of Width and Magnitude of Tornadoes

The level of tornadoes Magnitude is demonstrated by the size of circle, the length of the width is demonstrated by the sequential from light to grey.

We can observe that most of the tornadoes happened in the midwest and south of the United State.

Also as we can observe that most of the Magnitude of Tornadoes is positively corresponded to width of tornadoes,

the larger the circle the darker the color in general.

We can see the tornadoes in the south have deeper magnitude and width compared to other areas.

Most of the tornadoes event happened in afternoon and evening.
